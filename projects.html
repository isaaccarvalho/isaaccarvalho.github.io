Digiescola

A website of Muriaé Secretary of Education, developed during the pandemic quarantine for the access of students and teachers of the entire city. The UX/UI was developed on Adobe XD and was inspired by the Metro UI, an interface created by Microsoft based in blocks for intuitive navigation for children and elderly people. The website was developed in WordPress.

Mais Maker

An UX/UI project developed in Figma for the “Mais Maker” project, an university extension program which teaches IoT, CNC and MIT App Inventor courses for the public schools of Leopoldina and the region.

Encautech

An UX/UI project developed in Figma for Encautech, a junior company that works in the development of projects and solutions in the area of control and automation. It is formed by students of the Engineering courses of CEFET-MG.
